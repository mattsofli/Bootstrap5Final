About Professor Oak

Professor Oak is a Pokémon researcher based in Pallet Town, dedicated to studying Pokémon habitats, evolution, and their bond with trainers. A mentor to many, he provides starter Pokémon to new trainers and helps them embark on their adventures.

With decades of research, Professor Oak aims to expand the understanding of Pokémon and their role in our world.
